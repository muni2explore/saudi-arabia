Wide Variety Of Logistics Services

Ocean Freight Forwarding

We operate with a modern and dynamic structure, supported by a worldwide network of agents and correspondents. Our ocean freight services utilize cargo ships for cost-effective transportation of goods across seas and oceans, though with longer transit times and weather-related considerations.

We offer full container loading service door-to-door or port-to-port through a single point of contact. We provide streamlined freight forwarding to book, arrange for pickup, and delivery, and manage shipping documents for virtually any origin or destination. We handle various types of cargo, including dangerous goods, small quantity cargo (LCL), and containers.
